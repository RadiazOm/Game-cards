In our third semester our task was to build a web application for people with a hearing impairment in the environment sport. I did this with 4 other designers, Dieuwe van Rijnswou, Ricky Ng, Olivier Schell, and me.

Before we did anything programming related we went and researched our target audience in order to get a better idea of how our target audience lives. We discovered that people with a hearing impairment sometimes have a language delay. This gave me an idea what to build for these people. I decided on building a text-to-sign language translator in order to teach and better communicate with people with a hearing impairment.
